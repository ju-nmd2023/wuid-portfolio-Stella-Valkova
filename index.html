Originally I come from Bulgaria and I lived there my whole life before deciding to move to Sweden for university. In Sofia, the capital of Bulgaria I graduaded from the High School of Digital Sciences SoftUni Svetlina with specialisation in Graphic Design.

My greatest achievement during my years in High School is that I won first place in a national Web Design competition in 2022. However, the thing I am most proud of is that I started working as a part-time Graphic designer at the age of 17 for an American company. Because I studied 5 years Graphic Design I already have a diverse portfolio with many projects behind my back.

Apart from that, I describe myself as a very hard working, passionate and postive person. I enjoy teamowrking and I think I am a great communicator.
